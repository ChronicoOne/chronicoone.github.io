Games

Play fun open-source Chronico web games right in your browser.

Little JumpsPlay as a little jumper who desperately wants to avoid the black bars falling from the sky. This is an "HTML5 game" written mostly using JavaScript and should run on almost any modern web-browsing device.

Instructions: Press and hold (tap, click, or spacebar) to charge up a jump. Release to launch the Little Jumper towards your cursor.
